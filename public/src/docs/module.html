A parallax experiment

We are trying to explore the possibilities of having an interaction between the user and the content of a webpage solely based on the scroll movement. The most obvious technique to achieve scroll-based interaction is to implement parallax effect on the webpage. We have seen great examples of parallax animation:

  • nytimes
  • dropbox
  • stangelove

Let's try something different.

Though, unfortunately the demo shown above is just a way of playing around with parallax techniques. The more relevant interaction has yet to be created. Therefore the next demo will include a more viable interactive element.
